## <span data-ttu-id="ad761-110">設定環境</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-110">Setting up an environment</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-111">HoloLens 裝置知道如何透過*追蹤*空間中的使用者來放置穩定和準確的全像投影。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-111">HoloLens devices know how to place stable and accurate holograms by *tracking* users in a space.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-112">如果沒有適當的追蹤，裝置無法了解環境或其中的使用者，因此全像投影可能出現在錯誤的位置，不會每次都出現在同一位置，甚至根本不顯示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-112">Without proper tracking, the device does not understand the environment or the user within it so holograms can appear in the wrong places, not appear in the same spot every time, or not appear at all.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-113">用於追蹤使用者的資料在*空間地圖*中表示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-113">The data used to track users is represented in the *spatial map*.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-114">追蹤效能深受使用者所在環境的影響，因此調整環境以誘導穩定和一致的追蹤是一門藝術，而不是一門科學。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-114">Tracking performance is heavily influenced by the environment the user is in, and tuning an environment to induce stable and consistent tracking is an art rather than a science.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-115">追蹤技術融合了諸多不同的環境因素，但身為混合實境開發人員，您可以記住幾個因素，來調整更利於追蹤的空間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-115">Many different environmental factors are fused together to enable tracking, but as a Mixed Reality developer, there are several factors you can keep in mind to tune a space for better tracking.</span></span>

### <span data-ttu-id="ad761-116">光源</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-116">Lighting</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-117">Windows Mixed Reality 採用可見光來追蹤使用者的位置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-117">Windows Mixed Reality uses visual light to track the user's location.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-118">當環境太亮時，攝影機可能會過度飽和，什麼也看不到。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-118">When an environment is too bright, the cameras can get saturated, and nothing is seen.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-119">如果環境太暗，攝影機無法獲取足夠的資訊，什麼也看不到。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-119">If the environment is too dark, the cameras cannot pick up enough information, and nothing is seen.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-120">照明應該均勻且夠亮，可以輕易看到人，但也不要亮到太刺眼。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-120">Lighting should be even and sufficiently bright that a human can see without effort, but not so bright that the light is painful to look at.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-121">昏暗區域中使用點光源也會有問題，因為攝影機在進出明亮空間時必須進行調整。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-121">Areas where there are points of bright light in an overall dim area are also problematic, as the camera has to adjust when moving in and out of bright spaces.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-122">這可能導致裝置「遺失」，並認為光的變化等同於位置的變化。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-122">This can cause the device to "get lost" and think that the change in light equates to a change in location.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-123">區域中穩定的照明水準可提供更好的追蹤。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-123">Stable light levels in an area will lead to better tracking.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-124">室外光源也會導致追蹤器不穩定，因為陽光可能會隨時間變化很大。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-124">Any outdoor lighting can also cause instability in the tracker, as the sun may vary considerably over time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-125">例如，在夏季與冬季的同一空間進行追蹤會產生截然不同的結果，因為一年中不同時間，室外光線可能不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-125">For example, tracking in the same space in the summer vs. winter can produce drastically different results, as the secondhand light outside may be higher at different times of year.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-126">如果您有照度計，穩定的 500-1000 勒克斯是一個很好的起點。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-126">If you have a luxmeter, a steady 500-1000 lux is a good place to start.</span></span>  

#### <span data-ttu-id="ad761-127">光源的類型</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-127">Types of lighting</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-128">空間中的不同光源類型也會影響追蹤。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-128">Different types of light in a space can also influence tracking.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-129">如果交流電頻率是 50Hz，然後燈光脈衝也是 50Hz，則使用交流電的燈泡脈衝會貫穿它。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-129">Light bulbs pulse with the AC electricity running through it - if the AC frequency is 50Hz, then the light pulses at 50Hz.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-130">人類不會注意到這種脈衝。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-130">For a human, this pulsing is not noticed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-131">然而，HoloLens 的 30fps 攝影機會察覺這些變化 - 一些畫面格的光線很充足、一些畫面格的光線不足，而因為攝影機將試圖補償光脈衝，因此有些畫面格將過度曝光。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-131">However, HoloLens' 30fps camera sees these changes - some frames will be well-lit, some will be poorly lit, and some will be over-exposed as the camera tries to compensate for light pulses.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-132">在美國，電力頻率標準為 60Hz，因此燈泡脈衝與 HoloLens 的畫面播放速率協調 - 60Hz 脈衝與 HoloLens 的 30 FPS 畫面播放速率保持一致。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-132">In the USA, electricity frequency standard is 60Hz, so light bulb pulses are harmonized with HoloLens' framerate - 60Hz pulses align with HoloLens' 30 FPS framerate.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-133">但是，許多國家/地區的交流電頻率標準為 50Hz，這表示某些 HoloLens 畫面格將在脈衝期間拍攝，而其他畫面格不會。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-133">However, many countries have an AC frequency standard of 50Hz, which means some HoloLens frames will be taken during pulses, and others will not.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-134">特別是，我們已知歐洲的螢光燈會造成問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-134">In particular, fluorescent lighting in Europe has been known to cause issues.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-135">您可以嘗試幾件事來解決閃爍問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-135">There are a few things you can try to resolve flickering issues.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-136">溫度、燈泡年齡和預熱週期是螢光燈閃爍的常見原因，更換燈泡可能會有幫助。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-136">Temperature, bulb age, and warm-up cycles are common causes of fluorescent flickering and replacing bulbs may help.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-137">旋緊燈泡，確保電流消耗恒定也會有幫助。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-137">Tightening bulbs and making sure current draws are constant can also help.</span></span>  

### <span data-ttu-id="ad761-138">空間中的項目</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-138">Items in a space</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-139">HoloLens 使用獨特的環境地標 (也稱為*特徵*) 在空間中定位自己。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-139">HoloLens uses unique environmental landmarks, also known as *features*, to locate itself in a space.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-140">裝置幾乎永遠無法在缺乏特徵的區域進行追蹤，因為裝置無法知道自己位在空間中的哪裡。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-140">A device can almost never track in a feature-poor area, as the device has no way of knowing where in space it is.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-141">向空間的牆壁新增特徵通常是改進追蹤的好方法。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-141">Adding features to the walls of a space is usually a good way to improve tracking.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-142">海報、貼在牆上的符號、盆栽、特殊物品或其他類似物品都會有所幫助。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-142">Posters, symbols taped to a wall, plants, unique objects, or other similar items all help.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-143">淩亂的辦公桌是導致良好追蹤的一個很好的環境例子 - 單一區域中有很多不同的特徵。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-143">A messy desk is a good example of an environment that leads to good tracking - there are a lot of different features in a single area.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-144">此外，同一空間中請使用唯一特徵。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-144">Additionally, use unique features in the same space.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-145">例如，牆上有多張相同海報會導致裝置混淆，因為 HoloLens 無法得知它正在看著重複海報的哪一張。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-145">The same poster repeated multiple times over a wall, for example, will cause device confusion as the HoloLens won't know which of the repetitive posters it is looking at.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-146">新增唯一特徵的一種常見方法是使用不透光膠帶，沿著空間的牆壁和地板創造唯一的、非重複的圖案。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-146">One common way of adding unique features is to use lines of masking tape to create unique, non-repetitive patterns along the walls and floor of a space.</span></span>

#### <span data-ttu-id="ad761-149">蟲洞</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-149">Wormholes</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-150">如果您有兩個區域或地區看起來一樣，追蹤器可能會認為它們是相同的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-150">If you have two areas or regions that look the same, the tracker may think they are the same.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-151">這會導致裝置欺騙自己，以為它在其他地方。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-151">This results in the device tricking itself into thinking it is somewhere else.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-152">我們稱這種重複區域為*蟲洞*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-152">We call these types of repetitive areas *wormholes*.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-153">為了避免蟲洞，請儘量避免同一空間中出現相同區域。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-153">To prevent wormholes, try to prevent identical areas in the same space.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-154">相同的區域可能包括工廠機台、建築物上的窗子、伺服器機架或工作站。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-154">Identical areas can sometimes include factory stations, windows on a building, server racks, or work stations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-155">標記區域或為每個類似區域新增唯一特徵，有助於減少蟲洞。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-155">Labelling areas or adding unique features to each similar-looking areas can help mitigate wormholes.</span></span>

### <span data-ttu-id="ad761-156">空間中的移動</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-156">Movement in a space</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-157">如果您的環境不斷轉變與變化，裝置將沒有穩定的特徵可供定位。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-157">If your environment is constantly shifting and changing, the device has no stable features to locate against.</span></span>

### <span data-ttu-id="ad761-164">使用者與空間中項目的近接性</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-164">Proximity of the user to items in the space</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-165">就像人類對於極靠近眼睛的物體無法很好地對焦，當物體靠近攝影機時 HoloLens 也會出現問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-165">Similarly to how humans cannot focus well on objects close to the eyes, HoloLens struggles when objects are close to it's cameras.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-166">如果物體太靠近，兩個攝影機無法同時看到，或者如果一個物體擋住了一個攝影機，則裝置在追蹤物體時會出現更多問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-166">If an object is too close to be seen with both cameras, or if an object is blocking one camera, the device will have far more issues with tracking against the object.</span></span>

### <span data-ttu-id="ad761-168">空間中的表面</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-168">Surfaces in a space</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-172">不閃亮的物體較容易追蹤。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-172">Less shiny objects are easier to track against.</span></span>

### <span data-ttu-id="ad761-173">Wi-Fi 指紋考量</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-173">Wi-Fi fingerprint considerations</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ad761-174">只要啟用 Wi-Fi，地圖資料將與 Wi-Fi 指紋關聯，即使未連接到實際的 WiFi 網路/路由器也是如此。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-174">As long as Wi-Fi is enabled, map data will be correlated with a Wi-Fi fingerprint, even when not connected to an actual WiFi network/router.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-175">如果沒有 Wi-Fi 資訊，空間和全像投影的識別速度可能會稍慢。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-175">Without Wi-Fi info, the space and holograms may be slightly slower to recognize.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ad761-176">如果 Wi-Fi 訊號發生顯著變化，裝置可能會認為它處於完全不同的空間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ad761-176">If the Wi-Fi signals change significantly, the device may think it is in a different space altogether.</span></span>
