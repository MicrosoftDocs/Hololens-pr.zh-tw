<span data-ttu-id="83ce4-163">在 HoloLens 2 中，目視位置可讓您實現正確的全息圖定位、舒適的觀賞體驗，以及改善顯示品質。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83ce4-163">In HoloLens 2, eye positions enable accurate hologram positioning, comfortable viewing experience and improved display quality.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83ce4-164">眼睛位置會計算為眼睛追蹤結果的一部分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83ce4-164">Eye positions are computed as part of the eye tracking result.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="83ce4-165">不過，這需要每個使用者都能透過目視追蹤校準，即使在體驗不需要目視眼睛的輸入時也一樣。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="83ce4-165">However, this requires each user to go through eye tracking calibration, even when the experience does not require eye gaze input.</span></span>
